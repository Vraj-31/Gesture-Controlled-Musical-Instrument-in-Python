These are renders of the exact same procedural synthesis code used live in
Phase 2 (sound_synth.py) — not separate sample files. Listen to these to
get a sense of what the chords and drum hits actually sound like before
running the full webcam demo.

- chord_Em_down.wav  — Em chord, down-strum voicing (bass note first)
- chord_G_up.wav     — G chord, up-strum voicing (notes swept in reverse)
- kick.wav           — synthesized kick drum
- snare.wav          — synthesized snare drum (not yet wired into main_phase2.py)
- hihat.wav          — synthesized hi-hat (not yet wired into main_phase2.py)

snare and hihat are generated and tested in sound_synth.py already, ready
for you to wire into a fuller drum kit when you extend the drum-zone logic
in main_phase2.py.
